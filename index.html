Responsible AI

AI that knows
its boundaries.

Aggregated data only

The AI analyses aggregated financial totals — revenue, costs, balance sheet items. Never upload personal data such as names, salaries, or individual transactions. The system is designed for company-level financial intelligence, not personal data processing.

Aggregated · Company-level
Restricted by design

The AI is strictly scoped: it cannot access the internet, other clients' data, or external databases. It responds only based on the financial data loaded into your dashboard. No investment advice. No tax guidance. No legal opinions.

Scoped · Restricted
Human in the loop

AI insights are advisory — never automated decisions. Every recommendation is presented to a human who reviews, accepts, or discards it. The AI supports your judgement; it doesn't replace it. Compliant with EU AI Act transparency requirements.

Advisory only
No training on your data

We do not allow your API calls to be used to train or improve AI models — ever. Your financial data is processed in ephemeral sessions and immediately discarded. No logs, no retention, no sharing between clients.

Zero retention
Challenges, not confirms

The AI is built to challenge unrealistic budgets and forecasts — not to tell you what you want to hear. It flags when growth assumptions don't match historical trends and suggests concrete revisions.

Critical thinking
Transparent processing

You can see exactly which data the AI has access to and which role it's responding from. No black boxes. Full audit trail of which insights were generated and when they were pinned to reports.

Auditable
No API connectors

We deliberately do not connect to your accounting system via API. All data is uploaded manually as CSV or Excel — you decide exactly what enters the platform and when. No background syncing, no standing access to your books.

Manual upload · Full control
Data minimisation

Upload only aggregated financial totals — revenue, costs, balance sheet lines. The platform is designed for company-level data, not transaction-level detail. Less data in means less data at risk.

Less is more
Compliant by default

GDPR, EU AI Act (2024/1689), and EU Data Act compliance are built into the architecture — not bolted on. Data Processing Agreement, Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy are presented at first login and versioned for audit.
